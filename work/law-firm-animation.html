The Brief

A law firm needed a brand introduction video — something that would play on their website, at the beginning of presentations, and in digital communications. It had to communicate authority, trustworthiness, and professionalism without feeling dry or corporate.

The challenge: create something visually compelling that elevates the brand, entirely in 3D, from scratch.

The Process

This project was a deep dive into end-to-end 3D production — from concept through to final render. Every element was built, lit, and animated inside Blender.

01
Concept & Storyboard
Planned the visual narrative — what the brand stands for, how motion can communicate trust and authority, and the emotional arc of the piece.
02
Scene Building
Constructed all 3D elements in Blender — geometry, materials, textures. Every object in the scene was modelled or sourced and adapted.
03
Lighting & Camera
Set up three-point lighting rigs and camera movements that feel cinematic — slow, deliberate, confident. Light is the most important ingredient in 3D.
04
Animation
Keyframed all motion — logo reveals, camera moves, object animations. Every transition was timed to feel intentional and professional.
05
Rendering
Rendered the final sequence at high resolution using Blender's Cycles engine — balancing render quality with production time.
06
Final Output
Compiled, colour graded, and exported the final animation — ready for web, presentation, and broadcast use.
